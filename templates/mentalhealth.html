Mental Health

For someone to have the best possible life, they have an undeniable need to have good mental health. Unfortunately, it has been seen that mental health is not given enough importance in most cases. At the conception of Stonewall, one of the major challenges that was put up to challenge was the taboo about mental health.

Taking care of oneself and one’s loved ones in something that gets addressed more nowadays, which is a step in the right direction. For facilitating this upwards trend, the following is a resource to help you navigate the world that is mental health care.

Some Definitions

According to the World Health Organisation:

“Mental health is the foundation for the well-being and effective functioning of individuals. It is more than the absence of a mental disorder; it is the ability to think, learn, and understand one's emotions and the reactions of others. Mental health is a state of balance, both within and with the environment.”

In short, Mental Health is a basic trait of quality of living and includes the absence of mental health disorders, the ability to think, learn and understand emotions and the capability to gauge people’s reactions.
